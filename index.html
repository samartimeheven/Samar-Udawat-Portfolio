Project Overview

What is Toran?

Toran began as a platform for authentic Kolhapuri chappals and is now positioned to grow into a broader Indian handicrafts marketplace.

The brand connects skilled artisans with Gen Z consumers looking for products with craft, culture, and a clear story.

Project Overview

What is Shri Mandir?

Shri Mandir brings devotional services into one app, from puja booking and live aarti to daily spiritual content.

The challenge was to make a sacred digital space feel trustworthy, clear, and easy for both young and older users.

UI/UX Devotional Icon Design Accessibility

App Features

Online Puja & Chadhava Booking

Book pujas and offerings at renowned temples from anywhere.

Live Darshan & Streaming

Watch temple rituals and aarti live from the app.

Virtual Temple Setup

Create a personal temple space for daily worship.

Devotional Content

Aartis, bhajans, chalisas, and mantras in one library.

Astrology, Panchang & Horoscope

Daily tithi, festival calendar, and horoscope features.

Articles & Learning Material

Stories, guides, and learning material around Sanatan Dharma.
